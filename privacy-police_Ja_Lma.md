Privacy Policy — Ja lma
-----------------------

WahSoft built the Ja lma app as a free service. It shows the real-time tap-water
availability for the AADL 2700 housing estate (Aïn El Beïda, Es Sénia, Oran).
This page explains what data the app handles.

### What we collect
Ja lma requires no account and asks for no personal information: no name, email,
photos, contacts, or device location (GPS). When the app fetches water-pressure
data or the WahSoft product catalogue from our servers, our servers automatically
record standard technical data: your IP address, device user-agent, platform and
the time of the request. We also record anonymous usage events related to our
in-app promotions (for example: promotion displayed, dismissed, or tapped, and
product-catalogue openings), together with the requesting IP address.

### How we use it
This data is used only to operate the service, measure audience and usage
(visit statistics, approximate country/city derived from the IP address), and
improve the app. We do not sell it or share it with anyone, except the service
below. Approximate location is derived from your IP address on our server; the
app never accesses your device's location.

### Third-party services
- **ip-api.com** — used server-side to resolve an IP address to an approximate
  country/city for our visit statistics.
The app contains no third-party advertising SDK. The promotions shown in the app
are for WahSoft's own products only.

### Security
Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored on our own server. No method of
transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we apply commercially reasonable
protections and restrict access to this data.

### Children's privacy
The app is suitable for a general audience and collects no personal information
that would identify a child.

### Changes
We may update this policy from time to time; changes take effect when posted on
this page.
